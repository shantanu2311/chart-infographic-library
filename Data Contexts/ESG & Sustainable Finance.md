# ESG & Sustainable Finance

## Overview
ESG and sustainable finance data covers the disclosure, rating, and investment frameworks that channel capital toward environmentally and socially sustainable outcomes. This domain spans ESG ratings and scores, mandatory and voluntary disclosure regimes (TCFD, ISSB/IFRS S1-S2, EU CSRD, SEC climate rules), taxonomy alignment assessments (EU, ASEAN, China), green and sustainability-linked bond markets, impact investing metrics, and the growing regulatory architecture governing sustainable finance globally.

## Key Data Types
- **Scores / ratings** — ESG composite and pillar scores (E, S, G), controversy flags, industry-relative rankings
- **Categorical** — Disclosure framework (TCFD, ISSB, CSRD, CDP), taxonomy (EU, China, ASEAN), instrument type
- **Financial** — Green bond issuance, sustainability-linked loan volume, impact fund AUM, greenium (bps)
- **Binary / compliance** — Taxonomy-aligned revenue %, SFDR classification (Article 6/8/9), disclosure completeness
- **Time series** — Annual disclosure rates, fund flow trends, ESG score evolution, regulatory milestone timelines

## Key Metrics & Indicators
- **ESG scores** — Provider-specific composite and pillar scores (MSCI AAA-CCC, Sustainalytics 0-100 risk rating)
- **Disclosure rates** — Percentage of index constituents reporting to CDP, TCFD-aligned, or ISSB-ready
- **EU taxonomy-aligned revenue** — Share of a company's revenue from taxonomy-eligible and taxonomy-aligned activities
- **Green bond premium (greenium)** — Yield difference between green bonds and comparable conventional bonds (bps)
- **Sustainable fund flows** — Net quarterly/annual inflows into ESG and sustainable investment strategies ($bn)
- **SFDR classification distribution** — Percentage of EU-domiciled funds classified as Article 6, 8, or 9
- **CDP scores** — Annual corporate climate disclosure ratings (A through D-)
- **SBTi target adoption** — Number of companies with committed and validated science-based targets

## Primary Data Sources
- **MSCI ESG Research** — ESG ratings, climate indices, implied temperature rise, EU taxonomy data
- **Sustainalytics (Morningstar)** — ESG risk ratings, controversy research, corporate governance scores
- **CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project)** — Corporate and city climate, water, and forest disclosure responses and scores
- **Climate Bonds Initiative** — Green bond market data, taxonomy, certification, post-issuance reporting tracker
- **GFANZ (Glasgow Financial Alliance for Net Zero)** — Member institution transition plans, sector pathways, progress reports
- **NGFS** — Climate scenarios for supervisors, regulatory stocktake, greenwashing monitoring
- **ISSB / IFRS Foundation** — IFRS S1 (general sustainability) and S2 (climate) disclosure standards
- **European Commission / ESMA** — EU taxonomy technical screening criteria, SFDR regulatory technical standards, CSRD implementation

## Recommended Chart Types

### For Trends & Time Series
- [[Line Chart]] — ESG fund AUM growth trajectory, green bond cumulative issuance, or CDP A-list count over years
- [[Area Chart]] — Net fund flows into sustainable strategies over time with positive/negative shading
- [[Stacked Bar Chart]] — Annual green/social/sustainability/SLB bond issuance by instrument type

### For Comparisons
- [[Radar Chart]] — Multi-pillar ESG profile comparison across companies or sectors (environmental, social, governance pillars plus sub-categories)
- [[Grouped Bar Chart]] — CDP scores distribution across sectors or regions
- [[Heatmap]] — ESG score matrix with companies as rows and E/S/G pillars as columns, colour-coded by performance
- [[Lollipop Chart]] — Ranked disclosure rates across industry sectors

### For Composition & Breakdown
- [[Treemap]] — Green bond market by issuer type, currency, and use-of-proceeds category
- [[Stacked Bar Chart]] — SFDR fund classification breakdown (Article 6/8/9) by domicile or asset manager
- [[Donut Chart]] — EU taxonomy alignment of a portfolio (aligned, eligible but not aligned, not eligible)
- [[Waterfall Chart]] — Decompose ESG score change over a year (improved governance, worsened environmental, stable social)

### For Relationships & Flows
- [[Scatter Plot]] — ESG score vs financial performance (alpha, Sharpe ratio) across equity universe
- [[Heatmap]] — Cross-correlation of ESG rating providers (MSCI, Sustainalytics, S&P, Refinitiv) for same companies
- [[Sankey Diagram]] — Flow from regulatory requirement through disclosure framework to data provider to end investor use case
- [[Bubble Chart]] — Companies by taxonomy-aligned revenue % (x), ESG score (y), market cap (bubble size)
