# COP UNFCCC IEA

## Overview
This data context covers international climate negotiations under the UNFCCC (Conference of the Parties), Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs), the Global Stocktake process, and the scenario frameworks produced by the IEA and IPCC that underpin policy decisions. It bridges the world of diplomatic pledges with quantitative energy and emission modelling, tracking whether collective ambition aligns with temperature goals.

## Key Data Types
- **Scenario / projection** — IEA NZE, STEPS, APS pathways; IPCC SSP/RCP scenarios with probabilistic ranges
- **Policy / pledge** — NDC targets (conditional/unconditional), long-term strategies (LTS), sectoral pledges
- **Time series** — Historical emissions vs scenario trajectories, energy investment pathways
- **Categorical** — Country/party, negotiation track (mitigation, adaptation, finance, loss & damage, Article 6)
- **Gap analysis** — Emissions gap (pledged vs required), finance gap (committed vs needed), implementation gap

## Key Metrics & Indicators
- **NDC emission targets** — 2030 reduction targets (% below baseline year) by country, conditional and unconditional
- **Global Stocktake gap** — Difference between aggregate NDC outcomes and Paris-consistent pathways (GtCO2e)
- **IEA scenario pathways** — Energy supply/demand and emissions trajectories under NZE, APS, and STEPS
- **IPCC temperature projections** — Median warming by 2100 under SSP1-1.9 through SSP5-8.5 scenarios
- **Climate finance mobilised** — $bn/yr tracked against the $100bn goal and NCQG
- **Methane pledge progress** — Global Methane Pledge signatories and methane emission trends vs 30% target
- **Coal phase-out commitments** — Number of countries with phase-out/phase-down dates and remaining capacity
- **Article 6 credit transfers** — Internationally Transferred Mitigation Outcomes (ITMOs) authorised and transferred

## Primary Data Sources
- **UNFCCC** — NDC Registry, National Communications, Biennial Transparency Reports, COP decision texts
- **IEA** — World Energy Outlook (WEO), Energy Technology Perspectives (ETP), Net Zero Roadmap updates
- **IPCC** — AR6 (WG1/WG2/WG3), SR1.5, scenario databases (IIASA hosted)
- **Climate Analytics** — 1.5C National Pathway Explorer, warming projections, sectoral benchmarks
- **Climate Action Tracker (CAT)** — NDC assessment, policy evaluation, warming thermometer, country pages
- **Carbon Brief** — COP coverage, country profiles, interactive scenario tools, policy trackers
- **WRI / Climate Watch** — NDC Explorer, historical emissions, Paris Agreement tracker
- **IIASA AR6 Scenario Database** — Full scenario ensemble with energy, emissions, land use, and economic variables

### For Trends & Time Series
- [[Line Chart]] — IEA NZE vs STEPS vs APS emission trajectories to 2050 with historical baseline
- [[Area Chart]] — IPCC scenario envelopes (SSP1-1.9 through SSP5-8.5) showing warming range over time
- [[Stacked Bar Chart]] — Global energy mix evolution under each IEA scenario (fossil vs clean)

### For Comparisons
- [[Grouped Bar Chart]] — NDC 2030 targets vs current policy projections for top 10 emitters
- [[Lollipop Chart]] — Ranked ambition gap by country (difference between NDC and Paris-aligned pathway)
- [[Slope Chart]] — Shift in NDC ambition between first submission and latest update
- [[Table with conditional formatting]] — COP decision matrix across negotiation tracks

### For Composition & Breakdown
- [[Waterfall Chart]] — Decompose the emissions gap: current levels minus reductions from NDCs, policies, and net-zero pledges
- [[Funnel Chart]] — Progression from 195 Paris signatories to submitted NDCs to net-zero laws to on-track countries
- [[Treemap]] — NDC targets weighted by country emission share showing ambition clustering
- [[Donut Chart]] — Share of global emissions covered by different types of carbon pricing and regulatory instruments

### For Relationships & Flows
- [[Sankey Diagram]] — Flow from energy source through sector to end-use emission under different scenarios
- [[Scatter Plot]] — Country NDC ambition (x-axis, % reduction) vs implementation readiness (y-axis, policy score)
- [[Timeline Infographic]] — Key milestones from Kyoto (1997) through Paris (2015) to COP30 (2025) with annotations
- [[Bubble Chart]] — Countries by GDP (x), emission intensity (y), NDC ambition (colour), population (bubble size)
