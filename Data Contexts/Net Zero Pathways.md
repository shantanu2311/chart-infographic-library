# Net Zero Pathways

## Overview
Net zero pathways data tracks the targets, transition plans, interim milestones, and progress of countries, corporations, cities, and sectors toward achieving net-zero greenhouse gas emissions. This domain connects top-down scenario modelling (IEA NZE, IPCC SR1.5 pathways) with bottom-up target tracking, covering residual emission budgets, carbon dioxide removal (CDR) requirements, and alignment assessment methodologies like those from SBTi, GFANZ, and the Net Zero Tracker.

## Key Data Types
- **Targets / pledges** — Net-zero year, interim milestones (2030, 2035, 2040), scope coverage (1/2/3)
- **Scenario pathways** — Quantified emission trajectories under 1.5C and 2C scenarios with technology assumptions
- **Categorical** — Entity type (country, company, city, region), sector, target status (law, policy, pledge, discussion)
- **Gap / alignment** — Temperature alignment scores, overshoot probability, pathway deviation metrics
- **CDR / offsets** — Projected residual emissions requiring removal, DAC/BECCS/nature-based capacity

## Key Metrics & Indicators
- **Net-zero target year** — Declared year for achieving net-zero by entity (country, company, city)
- **Interim reduction targets** — Percentage reduction by 2030 and 2035 relative to baseline year
- **Residual emissions** — Projected GtCO2e remaining at target year requiring CDR or offsets
- **CDR volume required** — GtCO2/yr of carbon dioxide removal needed in net-zero scenarios (IPCC range: 5-16 GtCO2/yr by 2050)
- **Alignment / temperature score** — Implied temperature rise if all entities performed like this one (e.g., SBTi, MSCI)
- **Target credibility rating** — Net Zero Tracker assessment (achieved, head start, in place, not yet rated)
- **SBTi validated targets** — Number of companies with validated near-term and net-zero targets
- **Sector-specific benchmarks** — Emission intensity pathways for power, steel, cement, transport, buildings

## Primary Data Sources
- **IEA Net Zero by 2050 Roadmap** — Milestone-based pathway for global energy system transformation
- **IPCC SR1.5 / AR6 WG3** — 1.5C-consistent pathways, carbon budget, CDR scenarios, sectoral chapters
- **SBTi (Science Based Targets initiative)** — Corporate target validation, Net-Zero Standard, sector guidance
- **Net Zero Tracker** — Database of net-zero targets by country, region, city, and company with credibility assessment
- **NGFS (Network for Greening the Financial System)** — Climate scenarios for financial sector transition planning
- **Climate Action Tracker** — Country-level net-zero target assessment and sufficiency ratings
- **TPI (Transition Pathway Initiative)** — Company-level carbon performance benchmarking by sector
- **GFANZ** — Financial institution transition plan framework, sector-specific decarbonisation pathways

## Recommended Chart Types

### For Trends & Time Series
- [[Line Chart]] — Emission pathways from present to 2050 under different net-zero scenarios with overshoot bands
- [[Area Chart]] — Stacked residual emissions composition (energy, industry, agriculture, CDR offsets) converging to net zero
- [[Stacked Area Chart]] — Technology contribution to emission reductions over time (efficiency, renewables, electrification, CCUS, CDR)

### For Comparisons
- [[Grouped Bar Chart]] — Compare net-zero target years across G20 countries or top 50 companies in a sector
- [[Lollipop Chart]] — Ranked interim 2030 targets by ambition level for major economies
- [[Slope Chart]] — Shift in corporate alignment scores between two assessment periods
- [[Dumbbell Chart]] — Gap between current trajectory and net-zero-aligned pathway for key sectors

### For Composition & Breakdown
- [[Waterfall Chart]] — Decompose the journey from current emissions to net zero: efficiency, fuel switch, electrification, CCUS, CDR
- [[Funnel Chart]] — From total entities with pledges to those with plans, interim targets, and on-track performance
- [[Stacked Bar Chart]] — CDR requirement by method (afforestation, BECCS, DAC, enhanced weathering, ocean-based)
- [[Gantt Chart]] — Timeline of net-zero target years by country/company with interim milestone markers

### For Relationships & Flows
- [[Scatter Plot]] — Current emission intensity (x) vs target ambition (y) by company, coloured by sector
- [[Bubble Chart]] — Countries by emissions (x), GDP (y), net-zero target year (colour), population (bubble size)
- [[Sankey Diagram]] — Flow from current energy system through transition levers to net-zero end state
- [[Radar Chart]] — Multi-criteria assessment of a country's net-zero plan (ambition, policy, finance, CDR, equity)

## Example Visualizations
- **IEA Net Zero Roadmap** milestone chart showing key actions needed by decade across sectors
