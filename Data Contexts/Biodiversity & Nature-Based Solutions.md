# Biodiversity & Nature-Based Solutions

## Overview

Biodiversity and nature-based solutions (NbS) span the conservation, restoration, and sustainable management of ecosystems to address climate change, food security, and disaster risk. This domain includes tropical deforestation monitoring, REDD+ (Reducing Emissions from Deforestation and Forest Degradation), blue carbon ecosystems (mangroves, seagrass, salt marshes), protected area coverage, and the emerging market for nature/biodiversity credits. The Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework (GBF) set the "30x30" target: 30% of land and ocean protected by 2030.

## Key Data Types

- Forest cover and deforestation alerts (hectares, tree cover loss by driver)
- Protected area boundaries and management effectiveness scores
- Species population indices and IUCN Red List status
- Nature credit / biodiversity credit issuance and retirement data
- REDD+ project registries and verified emission reductions
- Blue carbon ecosystem extent and carbon stock estimates
- NbS investment and finance flow data
- Ecosystem service valuation (pollination, water purification, flood protection)

## Key Metrics & Indicators

- Annual tree cover loss (Mha) and primary forest loss
- Protected area coverage (% of land and marine area)
- Living Planet Index (population trend of monitored species)
- NbS investment volume ($bn/yr) by source (public, private, blended)
- Nature/biodiversity credit issuance (number of credits, $/credit)
- REDD+ verified emission reductions (MtCO2e)
- Species threat status distribution (% CR, EN, VU, NT, LC)
- Mangrove and seagrass extent change (km2)

## Primary Data Sources

- Global Forest Watch (GFW) — Hansen tree cover loss, GLAD deforestation alerts
- UNEP World Conservation Monitoring Centre (UNEP-WCMC) — Protected Planet database
- IUCN Red List — Species threat assessments
- Taskforce on Nature-related Financial Disclosures (TNFD) — Disclosure framework, LEAP approach
- Verra / Gold Standard — REDD+ and NbS credit registries
- WWF — Living Planet Report, Living Planet Index
- OECD — Biodiversity finance tracking
- Blue Carbon Initiative / IUCN — Mangrove and blue carbon data

## Recommended Chart Types

### For Trends & Time Series
- [[Line Chart]] — Annual deforestation rate by country or biome over time
- [[Area Chart]] — Cumulative protected area coverage toward 30x30 target
- [[Step Line Chart]] — Policy milestones (CBD COP decisions) overlaid on conservation metrics

### For Comparisons
- [[Grouped Bar Chart]] — Tree cover loss by driver (commodity, forestry, wildfire, urbanisation) across regions
- [[Lollipop Chart]] — Top 15 countries by annual deforestation or by protected area coverage
- [[Dumbbell Chart]] — NbS investment: current levels vs estimated need by region

### For Composition & Breakdown
- [[Treemap]] — Global deforestation by country, sub-grouped by primary driver
- [[Stacked Bar Chart]] — Species threat status distribution across taxonomic groups
- [[Donut Chart]] — NbS finance split by source: public, private, philanthropic, carbon market
- [[Waffle Chart]] — Progress toward 30x30 target (% of land/ocean currently protected)

### For Relationships & Flows
- [[Choropleth Map]] — Deforestation hotspots, protected area density, or biodiversity intactness index by country
- [[Sankey Diagram]] — Finance flows from source (government, MDB, private) to NbS intervention type to biome
- [[Network Diagram]] — Ecological connectivity corridors between protected areas
- [[Bubble Chart]] — Countries plotted by biodiversity richness (x), threat level (y), sized by NbS investment
