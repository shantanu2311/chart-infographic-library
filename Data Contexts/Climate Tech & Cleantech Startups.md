# Climate Tech & Cleantech Startups

## Overview
Tracks venture capital, private equity, and growth-stage investment flowing into climate technology companies. Covers deal flow, valuations, unicorn emergence, technology readiness levels, sector allocation (energy, mobility, food, built environment, carbon removal, industry), and geographic distribution. Climate tech has become one of the largest and fastest-growing segments of the global venture market, critical for bridging the gap between lab-stage innovations and commercially deployed climate solutions.

## Key Data Types
- Investment transaction records (round, size, valuation, investors)
- Startup and company databases (sector, stage, TRL, geography)
- Fund-level data (climate-focused VC/PE fund raises)
- Patent filings and IP activity in clean energy technologies
- Exit data (IPOs, SPACs, M&A, secondary sales)
- Technology readiness level (TRL) assessments
- Accelerator and incubator programme cohorts

## Key Metrics & Indicators
- Total climate tech investment (USD bn/yr)
- Deal count by stage (seed, Series A, B, C, growth, late)
- Median deal size by stage and sector (USD m)
- Sector allocation (% of investment into energy, mobility, food, built environment, industry, carbon removal)
- Geographic distribution (% by region: North America, Europe, Asia, RoW)
- Unicorn count (climate tech companies valued >USD 1bn)
- Exit count and aggregate exit value (USD bn)
- Fundraising by climate-focused VC/PE funds (USD bn)
