# Climate Disclosure & TCFD ISSB

## Overview
Covers the evolving landscape of corporate climate disclosure frameworks including TCFD recommendations, ISSB standards (IFRS S1 and S2), the EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD/ESRS), SEC climate disclosure rules, and CDP questionnaires. Tracks adoption rates, quality of disclosures, scope coverage, scenario analysis maturity, and the convergence of global standards toward mandatory, auditable climate reporting.

## Key Data Types
- Company-level disclosure status by framework (TCFD, ISSB, CSRD, CDP)
- Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions reported by company and sector
- Scenario analysis descriptions and quality assessments
- Assurance and verification levels (limited, reasonable)
- Transition plan quality scores
- Regulatory compliance status by jurisdiction

## Key Metrics & Indicators
- TCFD / ISSB disclosure adoption rate (% of listed companies)
- Scope 1/2/3 reporting coverage (% of companies disclosing each scope)
- Scenario analysis quality score (basic / intermediate / advanced)
- Third-party assurance level (none, limited, reasonable)
- CDP response rate and average score by sector
- Penalty and enforcement exposure by jurisdiction
- Year-over-year improvement in disclosure completeness
- Transition plan credibility rating

## Primary Data Sources
- TCFD — Status Report and annual adoption surveys
- ISSB / IFRS Foundation — IFRS S1 and S2 standards and adoption tracker
- CDP — Global disclosure platform (climate, water, forests questionnaires)
- SEC — Climate-related disclosure rulemaking and EDGAR filings
- EFRAG — European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS)
- IOSCO — endorsement tracking of ISSB standards across jurisdictions
- PwC / EY / Deloitte — annual climate disclosure benchmarking reports
- CA100+ — Net Zero Company Benchmark assessments
