# Public Finance & MDBs

## Overview
Public finance and multilateral development bank (MDB) data covers the flow of concessional and non-concessional capital from sovereign governments, multilateral institutions, and climate funds toward climate mitigation and adaptation in developing countries. This domain is central to the $100bn/yr climate finance promise, the New Collective Quantified Goal (NCQG), loss and damage fund operationalisation, and the broader debate around grant-vs-loan composition of climate finance.

## Key Data Types
- **Financial flows** — Commitments, disbursements, approvals in USD (nominal and constant)
- **Categorical** — Instrument type (grant, concessional loan, equity, guarantee), theme (mitigation, adaptation, cross-cutting, loss & damage)
- **Geographic** — Recipient country/region, donor country, MDB headquarter jurisdiction
- **Ratio / derived** — Mobilisation ratio (private capital leveraged per $1 public), grant share, co-finance ratio
- **Temporal** — Fiscal year commitments, multi-year pledges, replenishment cycles (GCF, GEF, CIF)

## Key Metrics & Indicators
- **Total MDB climate finance** — Annual joint MDB climate finance ($bn), split by mitigation and adaptation
- **Grant vs loan ratio** — Share of grants in total climate finance flows to developing countries
- **Mobilisation ratio** — Private finance mobilised per dollar of MDB/DFI investment
- **Country allocation** — Climate finance received per capita or as % of GDP for vulnerable nations
- **NCQG tracking** — Progress toward the New Collective Quantified Goal agreed at COP29
- **GCF/GEF/CIF disbursement rates** — Approved vs disbursed funds and pipeline status
- **Adaptation finance share** — Percentage of total climate finance directed to adaptation (target: 50%)
- **Loss & damage fund capitalisation** — Pledged vs deposited contributions to the L&D fund

## Primary Data Sources
- **World Bank Group** — Climate finance data, IDA replenishments, IBRD lending, joint MDB reports
- **Asian Development Bank (ADB)** — Climate finance operations, sovereign and non-sovereign climate lending
- **African Development Bank (AfDB)** — Africa climate finance tracker, Desert-to-Power, Great Green Wall finance
- **AIIB (Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank)** — Climate finance share of portfolio, co-financing data
- **Green Climate Fund (GCF)** — Project portfolio, disbursement tracker, readiness programme data
- **Climate Investment Funds (CIF)** — CTF, SCF, GCAP allocations and results
- **OECD DAC** — Official Development Assistance (ODA) climate-related aid marker data (Rio markers)
- **Climate Policy Initiative (CPI)** — Global Landscape of Climate Finance, biennial comprehensive tracker

- [[Waterfall Chart]] — Decompose year-over-year change in total flows by source (MDB own account, co-finance, mobilised private)

- [[Choropleth Map]] — Climate finance received per capita by country
- [[Bubble Chart]] — Countries by vulnerability index (x), adaptation finance received (y), population (bubble size)
- [[Alluvial Diagram]] — Track how a single MDB's portfolio shifts across sectors and regions over time
